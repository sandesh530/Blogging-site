How to build a successful Business?

There are two ways to start/own your business
1. Think about an idea and start a new startup
2. Find a niche in which you are good and start a business.
• A startup is a new way to show your business online with a small amount of investment and not many including members
• Business is a traditional way of doing a business which run by your family members to get in with them and scale it to the next level by taking some money from your parents and finding a niche in which you are better.

Growth in Startup:-

They want to grow rapidly by serving each and everyone all over the world as they are in the tech industry

Growth in Business:-

They serve in a local market from where they belong and network their business by scaling up to the next level with their long term vision

Benefits in Startup:-

They have to look at more profit just to recover their debts from investors or stakeholders

Benefits in Business:-

They are not that much greedy about profit as they take money from family members or take a loan from banks so there are needs are fulfilled on a small margin

Leadership in a startup:-

They are the real leader's risk-takers, vision creators they can run any business as they are skilled to scale up and grow their business

Leadership in Business:-

Small businesses are often founded by people who used to get an opportunity to scale in any niche and learn by running that business usually failing and learning to get success

How to make a profit in business

Businesses whether they are a startup or small businesses are all here to make profits and make their business sustainable

1. Learn financials:-

To make money you should learn basic financials like being able to read a balance sheet, cash flow statement and income statement, revenue, expenditures, and many more You can't drive a car without taking knowledge about it. It's just like this.

2. Create a blueprint of business:-

Everyone has an idea of a niche in which they want to go and run a business, one should create a plan to get succeed with time, your business plan will only let you know how to make a profit or sustain your business or shut down your business.

3. Set goals:-

You can't think that you will start a business today and you will become a millionaire tomorrow, you have to be patient and understand time leverage. Set smart and realistic goals that you can achieve in a proper time frame, and focus on long-term growth to scale better.

4. Identify what's stopping you? :-

To make profits you know are you capable of getting paid? If it's something lacking in you or in your business which not getting you towards growth. Work on leadership skills, and know what your sales team is doing Is that anything limiting your business from making profits.
