Time Management

Have you ever thought that you are not getting enough time to not get your work done in a day? Everyone in this world feels this way every time whether you are a student, working parent, Entrepreneur, or any other human being in this world. You all have the same amount of hours in a day. Do you know? what separates us from Jeff Bezos and warren buffet. The answer is time management skills

What is time management?


The answer is you have to understand the difference between being productive and being busy. You have to accomplish your important first if you keep doing your daily tasks according to your to-do list, you will always skip the important task without focusing on it. That’s all about how you made your daily planner plan your day with your important tasks so that you can master the time management skill.



How to improve time management skills


This is true that you have only 24 hours a day. you have much time to accomplish your goals and achieve the life that you dream of. Just stop thinking of time is out of your control, you will manage it properly by using these 5 strategies to 10x your productivity in your daily life.



1. Focus

The first step is getting clear what is your ultimate goal and taking action for it. If the tasks that matter to you are not planned then you will never find a time to do them. You should not focus on an outcome but on the actions you are doing to get your ultimate goal. Once you have a direction to move In you will get a better sense of what actions deserve your attention and where should affirm them.



2. Set smart goals

Smart goals are realistic, achievable, and measurable in a specific period frame. You should create and design your plan to achieve in a specific time rather than telling yourself you will make 1 million by this year set a clear goal, 83,000 in a month, or 1 million in a year set a realistic period that you can achieve your goal between that period.



3. Learn to say no

One should learn to say no to improve their time management skills by saying ‘no’ to the things that not helping you reach your goals. When you say no you are not being cruel but you are adding value to your life or supporting your goals. You should say yes to something important like being with family, exercising or a little bit of enjoyment If saying no seems hard, think about a time when you say yes to meaningless things and later regretted it after doing it. In starting its tough to say no to meaningless things but by practicing you will make it and improve your time management skill.



4. 30-minute rule

Do You want to get in the unstoppable zone? Use this 30-minute rule method to do your tasks in that 30 minutes by setting an alarm, giving your all attention to your work without any distractions whether the alarm goes off, decide you are going to put the task down or finish it. Take a small break and get back to the task again repeat this process daily. Over time you will be able to work with this rule without any timer.



5. Outsource your task

Want to become the best version of yourself? Outsourcing your task is the way to focus on your larger goals and take the new challenges on your own to grow yourself. To focus on large and important goals of your giant list you have to outsource to someone small tasks to handle items on your to-do list.
